This article contains information for those who wish to request access to another employee's mailbox or calendar and laptop backup

Access Scenerios


Customers who are departing soon should provide access via delegation or sharing while they are still active and advise those requiring their mail to raise a request once they have departed.

Dear ,
If you require other employees/colleague to have access to your mail after you depart, delegate access to them prior to your departure and advise them to raise a request (ref: Link scenario - access to departed employee’s mail) after you have left to access your mailbox. This is required because once your account/mailbox has been terminated from HR/IT side, their access will no longer work. If you attempt to raise this ticket pre-emptively, it will likely not be approved as it is assumed that since you are still an active employee, you can provide the requested access

Follow the below link to self-delegate your Outlook mailbox access to another employee / colleague.
